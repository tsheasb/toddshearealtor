Meet Todd

Get to know me. Then let's talk houses.

Nine years selling in Santa Barbara and Ventura counties. Eight seasons on the volleyball sideline coaching local kids. Rounds and rounds of golf. Twenty-plus years with Melissa, a Goleta local. Talking houses comes up eventually.

The approach

Creativity, integrity, and a relentless standard for the experience. No detail is too small, no expectation too high.

With a background in general contracting and marketing, Todd pairs real, hands-on property knowledge with a sharp eye for design and a modern approach to the business.

His deep roots in the Santa Barbara Association of Realtors — where he served as 2023 President and remains a board member — make him one of the most connected agents on the Central Coast. That translates directly into smoother negotiations and wider exposure for every listing.
