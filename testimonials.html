Todd was absolutely amazing to work with. My wife and I were first time home buyers and had been looking for a house in Santa Barbara for about three years and not really having much luck. We met Todd at one of his open houses and the rest was history.

He made us feel safe and explained all the steps a first time home buyer needs to know in detail. We are living in our home happily and wouldn't have done anything different!!
